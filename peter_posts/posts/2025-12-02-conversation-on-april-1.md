Let me give an example. 
Early in my career, Joel Spolsky influenced me a lot.
His radical question still matters: “Can you make deployment in one step?”

He believed deployment must be so automated that anyone can press a button and ship the product. 
This forces you to build systems that make it impossible to deploy garbage 🗑️

It’s about 𝗳𝗮𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗿 𝗶𝘁𝗲𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻𝘀. 
Automation shortens the path from idea → result → feedback.
Fast iterations are a managerial idea — about shortening the managerial cycle: the time from task to feedback. Here it is combined with an engineering process.

And when you combine engineering + managerial thinking, 
you become more valuable. 
💰 And that always converts into money: company profit and your salary.
